We Love Cars

Tech companies and Car manufactures are slowly starting to become one in the same. All the major tech companies are trying to get in the car game and all the large car manufactures are trying to apply more tech to their lineup. With self-driving electric car on the horizon it's only a matter of time until programmers become the mechanics.

We believe this to be a pretty natural transition however, since software developers very often also love working on cars. The point of Code and Cars is to capture both the interest to crate and troubleshoot applications, websites and video games and combine that with the love of going 100 miles an hour in a big piece of exotic metal.

Both developers and car enthusiasts love to create things that express themselves and show off their talent and creativity through a public medium. Also, we both enjoy spending time on advanced troubleshooting with intense satisfaction from overcoming mentally challenging dilemmas.

This is a place where you can find both and help drive enthusiasm towards combining some potentially really cool future tech into an old muscle car creating a new retro world of cars. Or you can just stop at one place to get some interesting content on either or both. Either way, Welcome to Code and Cars and thank you for the support!
